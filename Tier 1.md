## Scheme of Tier-I Examination

| Tier | Subject                               | Number of Questions | Maximum Marks | Time Allowed                                                 |
| ---- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------: | ------------: | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| I    | A. General Intelligence and Reasoning |                  25 |            50 | 1 hour (with sectional timer of 15 minutes for each subject) |
| I    | B. General Awareness                  |                  25 |            50 | 1 hour (with sectional timer of 15 minutes for each subject) |
| I    | C. Quantitative Aptitude              |                  25 |            50 | 1 hour (with sectional timer of 15 minutes for each subject) |
| I    | D. English Comprehension              |                  25 |            50 | 1 hour (with sectional timer of 15 minutes for each subject) |

### Notes
1. Tier-I will consist of Objective Type, Multiple Choice Questions.
2. The questions will be set both in English & Hindi except for English Comprehension.
3. There will be negative marking of **0.50 marks** for each wrong answer.
