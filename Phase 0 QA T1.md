# 4. LCM & HCF

## HCF

Highest Common Factor

Largest number which divides given numbers.

Example:

```
12,18

Factors:

12 = 1,2,3,4,6,12
18 = 1,2,3,6,9,18

HCF = 6
```

---

# LCM

Least Common Multiple

Smallest common multiple.

Example:

```
4,6

Multiples:

4 → 4,8,12
6 → 6,12

LCM = 12
```

---

# Prime Factor Method

Example:

Find LCM & HCF of 24 and 36


Prime factors:

24 = 2³ × 3

36 = 2² × 3²


HCF:

Take minimum powers:

=2² × 3

=12


LCM:

Take maximum powers:

=2³ × 3²

=72
```

---

# Important Formula

For two numbers:

```
Number1 × Number2 = LCM × HCF
