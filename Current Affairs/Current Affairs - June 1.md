## Q1. World Milk Day

**Question:** Which country is currently the largest producer of milk in the world, contributing over 24% to global production?

**Options:**

- A. United States
- B. India
- C. China
- D. Pakistan

**Answer:** B. India

**Explanation:**  
India ranks first in milk production globally, driven by the success of Operation Flood (White Revolution) initiated by Dr. Verghese Kurien. World Milk Day was established by FAO in 2001.
