## Q1. Varya AI

**Question:** What is the name of the indigenous cost-efficient video-generation model launched under the IndiaAI Mission?

**Options:**

- A. Krutrim AI
- B. Varya AI
- C. Bhashini
- D. Maya

**Answer:** B. Varya AI

**Explanation:**  
MeitY launched Varya under the IndiaAI Mission. Using a distillation technique, it reduces video generation steps from 50 to 4 and can generate videos at approximately ₹0.48 per second.
